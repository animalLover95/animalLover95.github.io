FAQ

We realize that you probably have some questions. So, hopefully, here are some helpful answers.

  1. Is there parking at the venue? Yes, but it is limited. To avoid walking, carpool with a friend, uber from your hotel or airbnb, or take the provided shuttle from the downtown hotels listed on our travel page.
  2. What time is the wedding, and will it be indoors? 5 PM and yes everything is indoors!>
  3. Can I bring a guest? Please only bring a guest if it was specified on your invitation. If you are confused about this, text Britt at 318-446-0191!
  4. Where should I eat brunch Sunday after your wedding? Some of our favorites are Rouj Creole, Solou, Mid City Beer Garden, The Chimes, and Elsie’s Plate and Pies!
  5. What should I wear? The attire for our wedding is cocktail attire. Here is a helpful link if you aren’t sure what that means.
  6. Can my kids come? We know many of our family members have small children and you are welcome to bring them if you would like, however please be aware that this event is catered to adults and there will be live, loud music that will go late into the night. If you need an excuse for a parent’s night out, this is us giving you permission to get a babysitter!
